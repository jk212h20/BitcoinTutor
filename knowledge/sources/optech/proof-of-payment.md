# Proof of payment

/ home / topics / 


    Proof of payment
    
  

  
    




Proof of payment is a cryptographic proof that a payment was made. The proof may indicate the identity (such as public key) of the payer and the recipient, and it may commit to additional payment details (such as a detailed invoice), but not all types of proofs provide those features.

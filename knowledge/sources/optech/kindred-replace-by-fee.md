    Kindred replace by fee
    
  

  
    




Also covering Sibling eviction

Kindred replace by fee is the ability for a transaction to replace a related transaction in the mempool even if there’s no conflict between the two transactions.  The main form of it is sibling eviction, where one child of an unconfirmed transaction can replace a different child.
